AMKAL ESPORTS new merch drop photo session with Sergey Petrechuk

10.04.24

Esports is the logical extension of what we have always done. Our journey began in 2018 when German El Classico founded the media football club "Amkal," bringing together top YouTube bloggers with the mission of elevating local  culture to a new level. Breaking stereotypes  and inspiring those around us, we have created  a multitude of unique projects in the industry:  a full-scale partnership with adidas, a product collaboration with Beats by Dre, and team jerseys in FIFA Ultimate Team. And it all started with  simple street courts.
